Watch Out

Web app

This project parses through input privacy policy or terms and conditions text and shows the user the key terms that may indicate ways in which users' privacy is violated or protected. The website also has information about what the terms mean, ways in which privacy is often violated online, and how users can protect themselves.

This project was done for the class Privacy in Networks at Barnard College, Fall 2019.
